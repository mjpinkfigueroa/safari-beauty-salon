About Safari Beauty

From safari roots to modern luxury — this is our journey.

🦁

Born from Passion, Built with Heart

Safari Beauty Salon was founded right here in South Gate, CA with one simple belief: every person deserves to feel beautiful and confident. What started as a bold, safari-themed vision has grown into something even more exciting.

Our roots — the warmth, the character, the spirit — are still very much alive. You'll notice subtle nods to our safari beginnings, but now nestled inside a bright, modern, minimalist space that feels as good as it looks.

We specialize in hair and extensions because we believe hair is one of the most powerful ways to express yourself. Our stylists are trained, passionate, and ready to make your vision a reality.
